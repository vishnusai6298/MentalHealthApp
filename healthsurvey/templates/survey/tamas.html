Tamas

Tamasic types suffer from mental dullness, inertia, and lethargy. They have little motivation to achieve either spiritual or material goals. Emotionally, they have severe blockages and cannot express themselves harmoniously, easily falling into violence and delusion. Their lives generally remain stagnant, with little ability to change or improve themselves.
