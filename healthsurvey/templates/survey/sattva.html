Sattva

Sattvic individuals are peaceful, calm and concentrated in mind. They have good thoughts and intentions and spontaneously do good actions. They are considerate, compassionate and selfless, placing the needs of others above their own. Emotionally, they have much love, faith, devotion and contentment.
